---
lesson: 2
title: Cognitive Reframing
objectives:
  - Label common distortions (catastrophizing, mind reading, all‑or‑nothing)
  - Generate balanced alternative thoughts backed by evidence
  - Practice outcome tracking
tone: Brief, skills‑based, encourage curiosity
exercises:
  - Write the thought → label distortion → evidence → alternative thought
  - Plan a small test; record what actually happened
disclaimers:
  - Not a diagnosis; not a substitute for therapy
---

Reframing is not forced positivity; it’s balanced appraisal.
