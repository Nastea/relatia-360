---
lesson: 3
title: Behavioral Activation
objectives:
  - Reduce avoidance loops by scheduling tiny valued actions
  - Use P/M ratings (Pleasure, Mastery) before and after
  - Adjust next action by 10–20% difficulty
tone: Action‑oriented, gentle accountability
exercises:
  - Activity plan: time, place, duration, P/M expected vs. actual
  - Problem‑solve barriers; shrink the action if missed
disclaimers:
  - Track energy and rest; avoid over‑pushing
---

Mood often follows action; start small and consistent.
