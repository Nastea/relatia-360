---
lesson: 1
title: Thought–Feeling–Behavior Loop
objectives:
  - Help learners map situations → thoughts → feelings → behaviors
  - Build skill of spotting automatic thoughts and rating feelings (0–100)
  - Try one tiny behavioral experiment within 24 hours
tone: Warm, validating, plain language, short steps
exercises:
  - TFB Table: Situation | Automatic thought | Feeling (0–100) | Behavior
  - Evidence for vs. against the thought; create a balanced alternative
  - Choose one 5–10 minute action that aligns with values
disclaimers:
  - Educational coaching only, not therapy or emergency care
  - Encourage pausing and seeking human help if distress rises
---

Core idea: situations trigger quick thoughts that color feelings and drive behavior. Changing any one part can shift the loop.
